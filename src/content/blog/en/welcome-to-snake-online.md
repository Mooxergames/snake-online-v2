Snake Online started as a simple question: what if the .io game we played in browser tabs at school could feel as polished as the AAA titles we play on console today?

Two years and a thousand iterations later, we're proud to introduce the new Snake Online — built from the ground up for buttery-smooth multiplayer, served from servers in 12 regions, and dressed in 200+ hand-crafted snake skins our art team has been quietly obsessing over.

## What's new

- **Sub-100ms multiplayer** in twelve world regions
- **200+ legendary snake skins**, all earnable through gameplay
- **Daily and weekly tournaments** with real prize pools
- **Cross-platform progress** — start on phone, finish on desktop
- **A leaderboard that updates every hour**, so the climb actually feels alive

## What's next

We're already deep in the next chapter — clan wars, seasonal storylines, and a new arena map called *The Rift*. Subscribe to our newsletter (coming soon) or follow us on the channels in the [community page](/en/community) to see them first.

Welcome to the arena. We've been waiting for you.
